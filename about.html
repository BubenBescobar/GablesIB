About the Gables IB Diplomma Programme

Our Vision -

Coral Gables Senior High School International Baccalaureate Diploma Programme will teach, inspire, and motivate our students to become integral members of a global society through preparation for a post-secondary education and career.

Our Mission -

The purpose of Coral Gables Senior High School International Baccalaureate Diploma Programme is to encourage a more cooperative and internationally-minded society by providing our students with the essential skills and knowledge to become to become principled, caring and reflective high school graduates. CGHS IB will maintain a safe learning environment within an interactive, engaging setting while supporting the individual needs of our diverse student population.
